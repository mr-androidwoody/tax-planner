Dividend Mode Tests

Pay-out · Reinvest · Breach target · Tax impact · Two-person

Tolerance: £0.02 for pure tax function assertions; £1.00 for full engine assertions.
Key mechanics: In pay-out mode, dividends = GIA × yield, deducted from GIA before growth (ex-dividend), and always recognised as taxable income regardless of the spending target — any surplus above the target is swept to cash. In reinvest mode, dividends are zero, GIA is not reduced before growth, and no dividend income enters the tax calculation.
